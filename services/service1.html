About Our Computerised Eye Testing Service

Computerised Eye Testing at Nalanda Netralaya offers precise, non-invasive, and efficient diagnosis of vision problems and eye health. Using advanced technology, we provide quick and accurate assessments to ensure early detection of conditions like myopia, hyperopia, and more, ensuring personalized care for every patient.
